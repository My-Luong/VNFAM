HOW TO USE


the Vietnam National Fine Arts Museum is divided into 3 floors with 25 rooms. All of them have been converted into 3D model for virtual visitors to visit online. Each room has a separate link for users to click on.

Each floor have a number of rooms:

  • Floor 1: Room 1 to room 8.
  • Floor 2: Room 9 to room 17 and room E2.
  • Floor 3: Room 18 to room 24.

For visually impaired visitors, if you want to visit a specific room, you can do the following steps:

  • Click on the link of the webpage.
  • Press H on the keyboard to lookfor the floor containing the target room.
  • Press up and down errow to go to the room
  • Click on the link of the room
  • Click on the button under floors to turn on the presentation with a male voice.
